Get Involved

Partner with Us ; Support Us

OAY believes in and values partnerships and collaborations. We base our partnerships on an understanding that is mutually beneficial and purposeful. While maintaining close relationships with past and current partners, OAY seeks to build and nurture new partnerships with Government, donors, academia, private sectors and other like minded partners in the sector that promotes resources, information, expertise and knowledge sharing while making sustainable contribution in adolescents and youth wellbeing. Further, OAY is currently promoting localisation agenda and seeking cooperation with international organizations to coordinate localization activities and increase effectiveness of development interventions. Are you interested in supporting the great work of OAY and its staff and volunteers? Are you seeking an impactful partner in adolescents and programming in Africa? Then, OAY is the ideal partner. Kindly feel free to contact us to contribute to making a better world for young people via info@oayouthkenya.org

Become OAY affiliate organization

We base our reach on growing with affiliate organizations and stakeholders. OAY will continue to build its national reach through affiliate member organizations and members of ICPD Youth Coalition, The Advocacy Accelerator Collaborative (TAAC), and Scaling up Nutrition Youth Network, KAYSRHR and Mental Health Youth Coalition, targeting all the Kenyan regional economic blocs by 2023. Adaptive learning and locally led approaches is key to this strategy. OAY will continue to work with and through the affiliate member organizations, partners and stakeholders to increase services and opportunities to young people. Volunteerism and Talent Management

OAY works with expert and youth volunteers while investing in them to ensure that the team has the right talent, skills and competence. As a youth organization, we do not have to have the experienced staff but will build their capacity as the most critical resource in the organization. Also, OAY gives platforms to its past volunteers/staff and non-youth experts who may want to offer some support.
