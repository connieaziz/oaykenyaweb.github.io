Leadership Development, Civic Engagement and Governance

Africa has a myriad of governance , corruption and accountability challenges that impede meaningful youth engagement in governance, political and civic processes. We believe that Africa is well endowed with resources but governance deficiency that breeds conflicts, dependency and corruption can be addressed in our generation. OAY is supporting nonpolitical and non-partisan engagement of female and male youth through their empowerment, access to opportunities and meaningful participation.

Under this programmes, OAY seeks to promote meaningful adolescents and youth engagement and participation for better governance, accountability , peace and cohesion.

call to action on governance

We seek partnerships to support key interventions under;

  • Civic engagement to promote accountability especially around policy and budgeting, devolution , voting and democratization, economic justice and global processes.
  • Community peace dialogue programs to promote sustainable local solutions.
  • Open governance and open contracting programs especially in areas accelerating realization of SDGs and uprooting of rampant corruption.
  • Leadership development program to promote peace, security and good governance in line with ;
  • UN Security Council Youth, Peace & Security Resolution 2250.
